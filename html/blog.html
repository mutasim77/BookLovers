A book doesn’t need to be connected to all the titles on the list, only to the one next to it in the chain. This month we’re starting with Eowyn Ivey’s The Snow Child which I’ve not read but I gather it’s based on a Russian fairy tale about a childless couple who one day find a little girl on their land.
